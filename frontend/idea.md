3. 🚨 Crucial Advice: How to make it win (The "Live" Factor)
While your core idea is great, you need to be careful not to build a "static" tool. The entire theme of this hackathon is "Live Agents" and moving beyond the traditional "text-in/text-out" or "upload-and-wait" paradigm. The organizers explicitly state: "The era of static chatbots is over — build immersive, real-time multimodal AI agents." Furthermore, 40% of the judging criteria is focused on "Innovation & Multimodal UX."

If your app is just: Upload PDF -> Wait 2 minutes -> Download Comic Book, the judges might penalize it for not being an interactive "Agent."

How to pivot your idea to fit the "Live Agent" requirement:
Instead of a one-click generator, turn your app into a Real-Time AI Co-Creator/Creative Director.

Interactive Storyboarding: The user uploads the PDF, and the Agent immediately starts talking to them (using audio/voice). The Agent says, "I've read your story. For the first fight scene, should we go with a gritty Manhwa style or a classic 90s Manga layout?"

Real-time adjustments: The agent generates the first few panels on the screen. The user can use their microphone to interrupt and say, "No, change the villain's hair to silver and make the panel read right-to-left." The agent immediately updates the visual on the screen.

Multimodal Experience: Combine text (the story), images (the comic panels), and audio (the agent discussing the creative process with you) to create a deeply immersive app.
